Drop any song. AI strips the vocals in 30 seconds. Synced lyrics on screen, phones as wireless remotes. One-time purchase, no subscription, works offline.

## How It Works

1. **Drop** — Point Beltr at your music library. AI-powered vocal separation (Demucs v4) isolates vocals, drums, bass, and melody into 4 stems. Any MP3, FLAC, or WAV is now singable.
2. **Connect** — Guests scan a QR code on the TV. Instantly connected, no app install, no account. Queue songs, browse the library, and vote on what plays next from any phone.
3. **Sing** — Synced lyrics highlight word-by-word on the TV. Real-time pitch detection delivers live singing scores. Fade vocals for showtime or keep them for practice.

## Installation Notes

**Windows:** SmartScreen may show "Windows protected your PC". Click **More info** then **Run anyway**. This is normal for new apps.

**Linux:** The AppImage requires `libfuse2`. On Ubuntu 22.04+: `sudo apt install libfuse2`. Alternatively, download the `.tar.gz` from the release.
